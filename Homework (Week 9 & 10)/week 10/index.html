Country with the Highest Alcohol Consumption in 2019

The datasets used to produce All About Wine contains 130 thousand wine reviews including variety which is the type of grapes used to make the wine, location of production, winery designation and price of wine.

In the world map, 181 countries are coloured based on their alcohol consumption per capita. A darker colour indicates the higher intake of alcohol the people of the country takes. Among all, Czechia has the highest alcohol consumption per capita in 2019. This is due to price of alcohol, specifically beer is cheaper than bottled water as well as a unique drinking culture (Lapelosova, 2018).
